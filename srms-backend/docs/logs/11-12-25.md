  - Initially hardcoded OTP (`123456`) for admin email in `AuthController` for quick testing.
  - **Fix Needed**: Replace with proper OTP storage mechanism (cache or database table) with expiration.
- **Missing OTP Storage**
  - OTP is generated but not stored anywhere, making verification impossible for non-admin users.
